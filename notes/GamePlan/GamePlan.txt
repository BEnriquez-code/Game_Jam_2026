Game will be a single player momentum based space racing game against AI.
-Asteroids will limit how fast the players can go, and will slow players down when hit
-Bullets will slow down someone when hit
-Passing a ship closely will give a speed boost, while hitting them will slow you both down drasticly
-Ships will have slight slip streams that allow players to gain a little bit of speed.
-Powerups if we have the time
